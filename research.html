Past Projects

Galaxy-Lyman-α cross-correlation in Thesan

In 2022 - towards the end of my first year of undergraduate studies - I did an internship at Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics, doing research on the Epoch of cosmic Reionization (EoR). The EoR is the epoch in the history of our Universe, where the radiation from the first stars and galaxies ionized the intergalactic medium. During my internship I used data from the Thesan simulations of the Universe during the EoR.
Publications: Paper I, Paper II


Chemical abundances in a planet-host star of the Praesepe open cluster

As a summer student at Nicolaus Copernicus Astronomical Center in Warsaw, Poland, I analyzed high-resolution specrtra of three main sequence stars in order to study whether exoplanets are associated with detectable signatures in the spectra of their host stars.
